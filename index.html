Welcome to TealDOS 0.1.0-alpha “Tallinn”

Born on April 25, 2025, TealDOS is a lightweight, elegant, and proudly retro-modern operating system. Inspired by the iconic Frutiger Aero aesthetic and engineered with minimalism at its core.

Download TealDOS 0.1.0-alpha “Tallinn”

Hosted via Mega.nz — fast, secure, and reliable.

System Requirements

  • Supported Architectures: x86 (32-bit)
  • CPU: Pentium-compatible or newer
  • GPU: VESA-compatible graphics card
  • Minimum RAM: 64 MiB
  • Recommended RAM: 512 MiB+
  • Storage: 1 GiB is more than enough

Why TealDOS?

  • Retro-futuristic interface with true Frutiger Aero vibes
  • Lightning-fast on old hardware
  • Alpha version already shows promise in stability and speed
  • No bloat. No nonsense. Just pure computing.
